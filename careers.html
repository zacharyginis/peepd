We're Hiring

Founding Chief Marketing Officer

As our founding CMO, you will define how Peepd shows up — in every channel, every conversation, every campaign. You'll build the brand from the ground up and the team to execute it.

Equity-Based Compensation Remote-Friendly Founding Team Full-Time

What You'll Do

  • Architect and own Peepd's brand identity, voice, and positioning from zero
  • Drive user acquisition, activation, and retention strategies across all channels
  • Build and scale a world-class marketing team as the company grows
  • Lead content, community, PR, and growth functions under one cohesive vision
  • Work directly with the founders to shape product narrative and go-to-market
  • Establish KPIs, dashboards, and the data culture that makes marketing accountable

What We're Looking For

  • Proven track record building a brand from early-stage or zero — not just inheriting one
  • Deep fluency in digital marketing, growth loops, and community-led strategies
  • Strong storytelling instinct — you know how to make complex ideas feel simple and urgent
  • Experience scaling teams; you've hired marketers and know what great looks like
  • Comfort with ambiguity: you thrive when the playbook hasn't been written yet
  • Data-literate — you set targets, measure everything, and iterate relentlessly
  • Passion for reputation, transparency, and building trust online

Compensation

Equity-Based Compensation

This is a founding role. Compensation is structured as a meaningful equity stake in Peepd, reflecting the outsized impact you'll have on the company's trajectory. We're looking for a builder who wants to own what they create — not just execute a marketing plan. Specific equity details are discussed during the interview process.

Why Peepd?

  • You're not joining a team — you're building one. As a founding member, your fingerprints will be on everything
  • Peepd is tackling a massive, unsolved problem: making reputation transparent, honest, and owned by people — not platforms
  • Direct access to the founders and real influence over product, positioning, and company culture
  • Fully remote-friendly with async-first communication
  • Move fast, ship often, learn publicly — we build in the open
