How Peepd Works

Your reputation is built by the people who actually know you — not by an algorithm, not by yourself. Just honest, verified reviews from real people.

The Idea

But what do the people who've actually worked with you, been friends with you, or dated you think? That truth has never had a home — until now.

Peepd is the platform where your reputation is built by the people who know you. They leave honest, anonymous reviews. The community votes on accuracy. And your Peepd Score reflects reality — not a personal brand.

The Process

Four simple steps

From sign-up to a full reputation profile — it's fast, free, and straightforward.

01

Join Peepd

Sign in with LinkedIn or Facebook in seconds. We pull your public profile — your name, photo, and basic info — so people can find and recognize you. No passwords. No long forms.

02

Share Your Link

You get a unique Peepd profile link. Share it with colleagues, friends, ex-teammates — anyone who knows you well. The more real reviews you have, the more accurate and powerful your score becomes.

03

Reviews Come In

Reviewers rate you across 6 categories and leave a written summary. They're always anonymous — so they can be completely honest. Peepd verifies that reviewers are real people, not bots or fake accounts.

04

Your Score Updates

Your Peepd Score updates in real time as new reviews arrive. The community can vote on whether each review seems accurate — rewarding honest feedback and filtering out anything suspicious.

The Score

What is your Peepd Score?

A single number from 0 to 1000 that captures how the people who know you see you — across six dimensions that actually matter.

Your Score Scale

0 – 1000
0 Phantom 500 Trusted 1000 Legendary

Your score is a weighted average of ratings across 6 categories, adjusted by the number of reviews and the accuracy votes each one receives from the community.

Reviews that the community marks as Accurate carry more weight. Reviews flagged as Inaccurate have less impact. This makes the system very hard to game.

New accounts start with no score. You need at least 3 verified reviews for your score to appear publicly.

The 6 Rating Categories

Work Ethic

Do they show up? Do they hustle? Are they reliable when the pressure is on?

Reliability

Do they follow through? Do they keep their word and show up when it counts?

Honesty

Are they truthful, direct, and transparent — even when it's uncomfortable?

Character

How do they treat people? Their values, integrity, and how they act when no one's watching.

Intelligence

How sharp are they? Their reasoning, problem-solving, and ability to learn quickly.

Social Skills

Can they read a room? Are they fun, easy to be around, and a strong communicator?

Accuracy Engine

The community keeps scores honest.

Anyone can leave a review, but not every review deserves equal weight. That's where the community comes in.

Every review on Peepd can be voted on by others. If the community agrees, the review gets more weight. If they think it's unfair or fake, it gets less. This creates a natural, self-correcting system that makes score manipulation nearly impossible.

Accurate Votes

Reviews marked Accurate are weighted more heavily — up to 1.4× the base score impact.

Disputed Reviews

Reviews with mixed community votes get a Disputed badge — visible to everyone viewing the profile.

Inaccurate Flags

Reviews flagged Inaccurate by a clear majority are de-weighted and may be removed after manual review.

Privacy

Reviewers are always anonymous.

We built Peepd from the ground up so that reviewers can always be 100% honest, without fear of awkwardness or retaliation.

Full Reviewer Anonymity

The person you're reviewing will never know it was you. Your name, photo, and account are completely hidden from them. Always. This is non-negotiable.

No Indirect Clues

We don't show who has "viewed" your profile either. There's no timing trick, no breadcrumb trail — no way to figure out who left a review.

Real People Only

Every reviewer must have a verified LinkedIn or Facebook account. We filter bots, duplicate accounts, and suspicious activity before a review counts.

Abuse Protection

You can report any review you believe is malicious, false, or a targeted attack. Our team investigates flagged content within 24 hours.

You're in Control

Data Privacy

FAQ

Common questions, answered simply.

Tap any question to expand the answer.

Can I opt out of being reviewed?

Yes. You can set your profile to private at any time, which hides your score and reviews from the public. You can also pause new reviews temporarily if you want a break. If you want to remove your profile entirely, you can delete your account — all data is permanently erased within 30 days.

What stops someone from writing a fake or vengeful review?

Several things. First, every reviewer must have a verified social media account — no anonymous burner accounts. Second, the community votes on accuracy — a suspicious one-star bomb gets flagged fast. Third, you can report any review and our team investigates within 24 hours. Confirmed fake reviews are removed and the reviewer's account is penalized or banned.

Is Peepd free to use?

Yes. Creating a profile, receiving reviews, and viewing public profiles is completely free. We plan to offer optional premium features in the future — things like detailed score analytics and verified profile badges — but the core experience will always be free.

How do I get more reviews?

The easiest way is to share your Peepd profile link directly with people who know you. Copy it from your profile page and send it to colleagues, friends, or classmates. You can also use the "Request Reviews" feature from your profile to send a polite nudge to people in your network.

Will the person I'm reviewing know it was me?

No. Reviewers are always 100% anonymous. The person you review will never see your name, photo, or any identifying information. This is a core, non-negotiable feature of Peepd — we believe genuine honesty requires genuine anonymity.

How is the Peepd Score actually calculated?

Your score is a weighted average of ratings across 6 categories: Work Ethic, Reliability, Honesty, Character, Intelligence, and Social Skills. Each category is rated 1–10. The average is then adjusted based on community accuracy votes — Accurate reviews carry 1.4× weight, Inaccurate reviews carry 0.3× weight. The final number is scaled to 0–1000. You need at least 3 reviews for a public score.

Can I see who viewed my profile?

No. To protect reviewer anonymity end-to-end, we don't show profile view history to anyone. Even if viewing patterns could be used to infer a reviewer's identity, we choose not to expose that data. Privacy is more important than a feature.

When is Peepd launching?

We're targeting Q2 2026 for our public launch. Until then, you can join the waitlist to be among the first to get access. Waitlist members will also get early-adopter perks and the ability to invite friends ahead of the public rollout.
