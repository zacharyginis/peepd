The Process

Reputation built by the people who know you

Every score is 100% peer-generated. No self-editing, no gaming the system — just honest, community-verified truth.

01
Find Someone

Search for any person by name or email. Every person must join and opt in to a Peepd profile before they can be reviewed.

02
Rate & Review

Rate them across six core attributes — Work Ethic, Reliability, Honesty, Character, Intelligence, and Social Skills.

03
Community Verifies

The community can vote on every review's accuracy. Disputed reviews are flagged and weighted down. Inaccurate reviews hurt the reviewer's Peepd Score.

04
Score Updates

Your Peepd Score updates in real time. The more verified reviews, the more accurate — and trusted — your score becomes.

The Accuracy Engine

Every review is clocked for truth

Reviews don't just sit there — the community actively validates them. Every vote shifts the Accuracy Score, and low-accuracy reviews are automatically downweighted so your Peepd Score always reflects honest, validated opinions.

Our algorithm cross-references multiple reviews from different people. The more corroboration a review gets, the more it influences your score.

Verified Reviews Carry More Weight

Reviews confirmed accurate by 10+ voters are boosted in the algorithm.

Disputed Reviews Are Flagged

When a review falls below 60% accuracy, it's flagged and its score contribution is reduced.

False Reviews Are Removed

Reviews confirmed as false by community consensus are permanently removed and the reviewer is penalised.
